Global Digital Humanities Symposium at Michigan State University

March 16-17, 2017

Digital Humanities @ Michigan State University is proud to sponsor its second symposium on Global DH. Michigan State University has been intentionally global for more than 60 years, with over 1,400 faculty involved in international research, teaching, and service. For the past 20 years, MSU has developed a strong research area in culturally engaged, global digital humanities. The symposium seeks to strengthen networks of exchange among DH scholars nationally and internationally.
